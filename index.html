Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NeuroBit

What hardware is required to use NeuroBit?

NeuroBit works with most robot platforms that have a standard control board with USB, I2C, or UART interfaces. Our universal connector kit (included with all subscriptions) allows for easy integration with popular platforms like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and commercial robots.

How does the cloud-based AI work without internet?

NeuroBit caches essential AI functions locally on your robot, allowing for basic operations even without internet connectivity. When connected, your robot syncs with our cloud for advanced processing and learning. For mission-critical applications, we offer an optional offline mode with our Business and Enterprise plans.

Can I transfer my subscription between robots?

Yes! You can easily transfer your subscription between robots through our dashboard. Simply deactivate one connection and activate another. Your AI configurations and learning data can be transferred or kept separate, depending on your preference.

What kind of tasks can my robot perform with NeuroBit?

NeuroBit enables a wide range of capabilities including object recognition, navigation, voice commands, adaptive movement, and environmental interaction. The specific tasks depend on your robot's hardware (sensors and actuators). Our platform includes templates for common applications like home assistance, security monitoring, and automated transport.

Is my data secure on your platform?

Absolutely. We employ end-to-end encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your robot's data is isolated in secure containers, and we never share or sell your information. Business and Enterprise plans include additional security features like private cloud options and custom data retention policies.

Can I customize the AI behavior for specific needs?

Yes, our visual programming interface allows you to customize AI behavior without coding. For advanced users, we provide API access and the ability to upload custom algorithms (Business and Enterprise plans). Our Enterprise plan includes custom AI development services tailored to your specific requirements.
